Chocolate Chip Dessert Pizza

Servings

10 slices

About

The best kind of pizza ever made! Made with a simple thin crust, a cream cheese based “white pizza sauce”, lots of chocolate, and a crumbly streusel topping.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour (185g)
  • ½ teaspoon instant yeast
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅔ cup warm water (155ml)
  • 1/4 Cup Coffee liquer-mixed with 1 Cup strong Coffee
  • 8 oz cream cheese (225g)
  • ¼ cup sugar (50g)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mini chocolate chips (175g)
  • 3 Tablespoons butter melted
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 Tablespoons brown sugar tightly packed
