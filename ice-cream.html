Brown Sugar Peaches with Ice Cream

Servings

4

About

The sweet, fragrant stone fruits of summer are wonderful for making quick and easy desserts!.

Ingredients

  • 4 peaches
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 4 Tbs. (1/2 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
