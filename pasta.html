CHICKEN AND BROCCOLI PENNE ALFREDO: (FULL RECIPE AT BOTTOM)

Serving Size

400 g

About

This Chicken and Broccoli Penne Alfredo is easy to whip up for dinner and delicious! This has been one of my most popular recipes for years!

Ingredients

  • 3 boneless skinless chicken breast, diced or cubed
  • 3-4 cups broccoli florets
  • Olive Oil
  • 16 oz penne pasta
  • 2 sticks of butter (1 cup)
  • 2 Cup of heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons of white pepper (or black pepper)
  • 2cups of freshly grated Parmesan
  • Extra Grated Parmesan for garnish
